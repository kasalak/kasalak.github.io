About Me

Once upon a time, I was a financial auditor for the state of North Carolina, but through automating the generation of some reports for work, I fell into the rabbit hole that is the tech industry.

I took the Data Science track at the Iron Yard campus in Durham, where I learned Python and associated data science techniques using Python libraries and the Django framework. Most importantly of all, I learned to lose my fear of failure, which lead me to embrace diving into data and create meaningful relationships.

I ended up working at Windsor Circle, a Python-based email retention platform, where I quickly learned that in the fast-paced environment of a startup, the ability to "wear many hats" and work on concurrent projects while being able to prioritize and make deliverable deadlines is crucial.

Luckily, I was surrounded by passionate, intelligent people that share a 'don't think outside the box, shred the box' and 'teams survive longer than individuals' mentality. This led me to my current role as an Operations Analyst, where I can 'wear many hats' and still contribute to my company through reporting, improving process efficiencies, and resolving client issues.

I am looking for a role in the greater Philadelphia area. When I'm not working, I enjoy traveling (visited South America in March 2016), watching college/professional football, and working on my Cicerone certification.
